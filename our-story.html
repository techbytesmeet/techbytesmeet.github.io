Our Amazing Journey

Different ways we Engage, Educate and Empower Canadian Youths with STEM / STEAM skills:

  • Create year round opportunities for Canadian Youths to participate in STEM / STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics) competitions and events.
  • Provide mentorship and networking opportunities for Canadian Youths interested in STEM fields.
  • Make STEM education accessible to underrepresented groups by providing financial assistance and outreach programs.

STEM education is important for preparing the next generation of youth for the jobs of the future.

By teaching STEM skills, students will be equipped with the knowledge and abilities necessary to succeed in fields that are increasingly reliant on technology and innovation.

Encourage hands-on learning through STEM-related activities and projects, such as Programming (Coding), Design Thinking, Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and problem-solving skills.

Additionally, STEM education can help to foster creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration, which are important skills for success in any field.

Colloborate with local businesses and organizations to provide real-world applications of STEM skills.

Empowering the Canadian Youths with STEM skills involves providing them with the resources, support, and opportunities necessary to develop and apply their knowledge and abilities in STEM fields.

Additionally, creating a positive and inclusive learning environment, where students feel valued and supported, is also important in empowering youth with STEM skills.

This will help to create an environment where students feel confident and motivated to pursue STEM careers.

Show the potential of STEM careers and encourage students to explore the many different STEM fields available.

Provide industry exposure and internships for students to experience STEM in action.

We at Tech Bytes Foundation, provide a multifaceted approach that includes hands-on learning, mentorship, networking, and exposure to real-world Digital applications and Internship opportunities.

Science & Tech Event

Our Tech Bytes Foundation Science and Technology meetup held in Surrey, BC was well received with 100+ audience. 2 youths compered for this event, 9 youths presented their Tech ideas, 1 youth demonstrated a Science project and 3 youths did Tech workshops. Variety of Science & Technology topics were discussed.

Steam Challenge

Over the past 3+ years we have conducted several STEM/STEAM Challenges for youths and have recognized consistent performers with rewards. Overall we have received 3000+ Coding and Design STEM projects from our participants. Join us to develop your ideas into creative projects and win rewards!
