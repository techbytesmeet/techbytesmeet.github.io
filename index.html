Everything needs a beginning and if that happens at an early age, it’s even great.

At TechBytes, we would like to create a platform for young minds to discuss and debate about Science and Technology!

On Feb 23 2019, we are beginning this journey, come join us!!!

Presentation

We invite students to form a team of 2 or 3 and present their tech/science ideas as a presentation with more visuals not exceeding 10 mins.

Leadership

We will have some on the spot fun tech-related challenges/games to play which will need a leader to consult with the team and provide the final answer.

Product Creation

We would like to invite people who have created any tech/science related products/projects to provide a demo and share the knowledge.
