OUR GOAL

At Tech Bytes Foundation, our goal is to create a platform for young minds to discuss, debate and experiment about Science and Technology! We envision creating an ecosystem where youths understand the impact of technology is in every sector. Once that realization comes, we believe more and more youths will be willing to participate in the technology revolution happening around us in every field!

Engage

Create year round opportunities for Canadian Youths to engage in STEM competitions and events thereby improving their Technical & Communication skills through participation.

Educate

Provide STEM-focused curriculum, encourage hands-on learning, Collaboration and promote initiatives that make STEM education accessible to all Canadian Youths regardless of their background.

Empower

Create a positive, inclusive & creative learning environment, where Youths feel valued and supported to pursue STEM careers. Provide industry exposure and internships for students to experience STEM in action.
